About

Chetna Mehrotra grew up in Lucknow, India where she had her first experiences with yoga. As a young child, she would always watch her mother wake up early and start the day off with practicing asanas. She noticed her that yoga brought a calmness to her mother and even helped her with her arthritis in later years. These memories of her mother always remained with Chetna and eventually brought her to learning yoga herself.

Chetna is a Yoga Teacher Training Certified Instructror. She has recieved her certification through the YogaLife Institute. YogaLife has deep roots in the Yoga Institue of Mumbai which has ensured a focus on Classical Yoga.
