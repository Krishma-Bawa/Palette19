Home Design

My formal training and significant experience in fashion design and home design, helps me bring a unique perspective to your project. I think that good home design needs to deliver on several parameters. It needs to bring together the practical with the beautiful. A room needs to be functionally able to deliver its purpose. It needs to match the owners’ style and aesthetic sensibility. It needs to look trendy, yet be timeless. There should be no doubt that it increases the value of the home.

The role of the designer is to help the owner to find the perfect intersection of all these elements in a way that it becomes not a “signature Palette19 look” but the signature look of the homeowner.

Consultation services include whole house renovation, kitchen and bathroom remodels, individual rooms or floors, color consultation, furniture procurement and layouts.
